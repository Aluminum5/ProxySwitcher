Deploying the group policy templates to your domain central store or if the computer is not a member of a domain, deploy it to this location:
ProxySwitcher.admx	:	C:\Windows\PolicyDefinitions
ProxySwitcher.adml	:	C:\Windows\PolicyDefinitions\en-US

After that, the group policy editor shows a new "Proxy Switcher" node under:
Computer Configuration -> Administrative Templates
